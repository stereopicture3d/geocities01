I filmed various events at the recent NSA Convention in Richmond VA with a Sony HandyCam and then digitized these pictures with Snappy video capture.

Included among the photos below are pictures of:

  1. Special Byrd Theater showing of 3D movie "Dial M for Murder" including behind the scenes in the projection booth.
  2. Trade show people and items for sale
  3. Nearby Maymont House and Gardens in Richmond VA (beautiful!)
  4. Stereo Theater including a picture from the Smithsonian Collection and Tiger Woods
